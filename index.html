Rules & Regulations

Company Operating Rules

1.

Profit Shri Pvt. Ltd. executes all projects exclusively through partnerships.

2.

The company conducts business in compliance with applicable Indian laws and regulations.

3.

Every project plan is prepared in advance and submitted for approval wherever required.

4.

The company undertakes a maximum of 4 projects per year.

5.

Every project is completed within one year and required updates are provided to the concerned government department.

6.

A Shareholder ID is provided to every project partner as per partnership agreement and applicable laws.

7.

A 100% guaranteed profit plan is provided to business partners in advance.

8.

Partnership is entered only after verifying a valid Police Verification Report of the prospective partner.

9.

SR Fund will be used to promote the Oxygen Langar initiative through events and public awareness programs.
